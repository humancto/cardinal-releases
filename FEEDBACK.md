# Feedback for Cardinal

Cardinal is free to use, free to share, and free to keep. There's no signup, no telemetry, no analytics — which means **the only way I learn what works is if you tell me.**

If something helped your child: I want to know.
If something frustrated you: I want to know that too.
If you want a chapter that doesn't exist yet: tell me what.

## How to send feedback

Three channels, in order of preference:

### 1. GitHub Issues (most helpful)

Best for: feature requests, bug reports, accessibility issues.

→ **[Open a new issue](https://github.com/humancto/cardinal-releases/issues/new/choose)**

Two structured templates are available:

- **Feature request** — suggest a chapter, lesson improvement, or accessibility refinement
- **Bug report** — something didn't work as documented

Both have a built-in privacy reminder. Please don't include your child's name, photo, voice, or location.

### 2. GitHub Discussions (most welcoming)

Best for: general questions, parent stories, "this worked for my kid" wins, asking other parents.

→ **[Open a discussion](https://github.com/humancto/cardinal-releases/discussions)**

This is the lower-stakes place. No template, no triage label, just a conversation. Other Cardinal parents will probably reply alongside Archith.

### 3. Direct email (for sensitive stuff)

Best for: anything tied to a specific kid's profile, beta program inquiries, or anything you'd rather not say publicly.

→ **archith.rapaka@gmail.com**

I read every email. Response time is usually 1-3 days.

## What happens to your feedback

- **Public issues and discussions** go into Cardinal's roadmap directly. The ROADMAP is in the codebase repo at `humancto/cardinal/ROADMAP.md` — open and dated.
- **Private emails** I keep confidential. Aggregated themes might inform the roadmap, but no individual email is quoted, screenshotted, or shared without your written re-consent.
- **Aggregated patterns** across many families may show up in beta-findings documents (also public, no names). The closed-beta observation protocol committed in writing is at [`docs/observation-protocol.md`](https://github.com/humancto/cardinal/blob/main/docs/observation-protocol.md).

## What I _can't_ promise

- I won't ship every feature request. Cardinal is small on purpose — the seven-shape exercise predictability is a core commitment (PRD §4.1).
- I can't reply individually to every issue, but I read every one.
- I won't add gamification (streaks, badges, scoring, time pressure). Errorless learning is non-negotiable (PRD §4.4).

## Privacy commitments

- No audio, no video, no kid identifiers collected anywhere.
- No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry in the app.
- GitHub Issues are public — your screen name is visible. Use a pseudonym if you'd prefer.
- The Cloudflare Web Analytics on the landing page records page views only (no cookies, no PII, GDPR-clean per Cloudflare's documentation).
